Best Movies according to Adam

My Top 3 Best movies Of all Time

Spirited Away

This was the earliest animated movie I can remember watching. It showed me an interesting world
that at times fascinated me and at others genuinely creeped me out, and no I am not just
talking about Noface I am talking about the granny with a big head and a skin tag inbetween her
eyebrows named yubaba.

Treasure Planet

This movie introduced me to space travel. Although more fantasy with all the flying seaships than what
you would expect from a scifi movie it made me think about what else could exist out there in the void of space.

Drive

I loved the tone of the movie and it's beautiful music.
